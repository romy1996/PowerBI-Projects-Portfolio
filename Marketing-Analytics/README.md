# 📊 Marketing Analytics Dashboard (Power BI)

## 🔍 Project Overview

This project focuses on analyzing marketing and sales performance to understand business growth patterns and identify key drivers of revenue. The dashboard provides insights into campaign effectiveness, regional performance, and product contribution.



## 🛠 Tools Used

* Power BI
* Excel



## 📈 Key Analysis Performed

* Data cleaning and transformation using Power Query
* Data modeling and relationship building
* Creation of interactive dashboards for business insights



## 📊 Key Insights

* Identified top-performing regions and products
* Analyzed revenue trends across different time periods
* Evaluated marketing performance and its impact on sales
* Highlighted key contributors to overall business growth



## 💡 Business Impact

This dashboard helps stakeholders:

* Understand marketing effectiveness
* Track business performance
* Make data-driven decisions to improve growth
